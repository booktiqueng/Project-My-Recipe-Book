Jollof Rice

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Price
Rice 3 cups 1500 Naira
Tomato Paste 1/4 cup 300 Naira
Chicken 1/2 chicken 5000 Naira
Vegetable oil 1/4 cup 400 Naira
Spices (Thyme, Curry) To taste 500 Naira

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add tomato paste, spices, and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  4. Stir in the washed rice and reduce heat.
  5. Cover and cook until the rice is tender. Serve hot.

Nigerian Suya

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Price
Beef 500g 4500 Naira
Spice(Yaji) 4 tsp 990 Naira
Vegetable oil 5 tsp 500 Naira
Skewers 10 300 Naira

Steps

  1. Cut the beef or into thin strips.
  2. Marinate with suya spice and oil for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the meat onto skewers.
  4. Grill or roast until fully cooked, turning occasionally.
  5. Serve with sliced onions and tomatoes.

Egusi Soup

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Price
Egusi (Melon Seeds) 1 cup 500 Naira
Palm oil 1/4 cup 400 Naira
Stockfish 200g 3500 Naira
Spices To taste 600 Naira
Vegetable 2 cups 700 Naira

Steps

  1. Blend egusi seeds into a paste and set aside.
  2. Heat palm oil and fry the egusi paste until it thickens.
  3. Add stock and spices, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the spinach and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Serve with pounded yam or rice.
